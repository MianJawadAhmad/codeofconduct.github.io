Community code of conduct

Introduction

Our community aims is to bring out the talented geeks , to polish their technical skills and to push them out to market. we arrange workshops, events, code labs, events and hackathons for the community. we help final year project to give them a good envirment where the can work better on their projects. We provide place for our community members where the can work on real world problems to solve.

Our Standards

Every member of our community has the right to have their identity respected. Our community is dedicated to providing a positive experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, race, or religion (or lack thereof), education, or socio-economic status.

Expected Behavior

1) Be welcoming.
2) Be kind.
3) Look out for each other.

Unacceptable Behavior

1) Conduct or speech which might be considered sexist, racist, homophobic, transphobic, ableist or otherwise discriminatory or offensive in nature.
2) Intimidation or harassment.
3) Disrespect towards differences of opinion.
4) Inappropriate attention or contact. Be aware of how your actions affect others. If it makes someone uncomfortable, stop.
5) Not understanding the differences between constructive criticism and disparagement.
6) Sustained disruptions.
7) Violence, threats of violence or violent language.

Enforcement

1) Understand that speech and actions have consequences, and unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated.
2) If you are the subject of, or witness to any violations of this Code of Conduct, please email us a.jawad321@gmail.com.
3) If violations occur, organizers will take any action they deem appropriate for the infraction, up to and including expulsion.
